Welcome to Modern Picker!

Your ultimate tool for picking and managing colors with ease.

About Modern Picker

Modern Picker is a versatile Chrome extension designed to help you manage and utilize colors in a streamlined and efficient way. Whether you're a designer, developer, or just a color enthusiast, Modern Picker provides a comprehensive set of tools to help you pick colors, convert them between formats, and maintain a history of your favorite colors.

Key Features

  • **Advanced Color Picker**: Select colors from a color wheel, input color codes directly, or use the color picker tool to get the perfect color for your needs.
  • **Color History**: Save and manage your recently used colors. View your history and quickly access previously used colors.
  • **Copy Color Codes**: Easily copy color codes in various formats such as HEX, RGB, HSL, HSV, and more. Conveniently paste them into your projects.
  • **Manage Color History**: Delete individual colors or clear all history with a single click.
  • **Dark and Light Mode**: Toggle between dark and light modes to suit your visual preferences and working environment.

How to Use

  1. Open the Extension: Click on the Modern Picker icon in your Chrome toolbar to open the extension popup.
  2. Select a Color: Use the color picker tool to choose a color or enter a color code manually.
  3. Choose Color Format: Select your desired color format (HEX, RGB, HSL, etc.) from the dropdown menu.
  4. Save Color: Click "OK" to add the color to your history.
  5. Copy Color Code: Use the "Copy" button to copy the color code to your clipboard.
  6. Manage History: Access and manage your color history from the extension popup. You can delete specific colors or clear all history.
  7. Toggle Dark/Light Mode: Click the moon/sun icon at the top-right corner to switch between dark and light modes.
